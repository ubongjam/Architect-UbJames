Introduction

Welded connections provide a continuous path for load transfer between structural steel members. Unlike bolted connections, welding relies on the fusion of base metal and filler material at the molecular level, creating a monolithic structure.

architecture

Theory of Fusion

The effective throat of a weld is the minimum distance from the root of the weld to its face. For fillet welds, this is calculated as 0.707 × leg size (w).

Effective Area Calculation

A_e = t_e × L

Where t_e is effective throat and L is effective length.
